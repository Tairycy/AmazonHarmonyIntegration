Configuring the Hardware

PIC32MX470 Curiosity Development Board 

Configuration: pic32mx470_curiosity

  1. Ensure that a jumper is placed at 4-3 on J8, to select supply from debug USB connector.
  2. Power the PIC32MX470 Curiosity Development Board from a Host PC through a Type-A male to mini-B USB cable connected to Mini-B port (J3).

 

 

  1. Mount the SD Click board, "microSD click" from MikroElektronika (http://www.mikroe.com/click/microsd/) on the mikro bus interface J5.
  2. Plug a micro SD card into the microSD click board card slot.

 

  1. Mount the is PIC32 Audio Codec Daughter Card - AK4642EN (http://www.microchip.com/DevelopmentTools/ProductDetails.aspx?PartNO=ac320100) on the X32 interface header (J14, J15)

  2. Power the PIC32MZ EF Curiosity Development Board from a Host PC through a Type-A male to micro-B USB cable connected to Micro-B port (J3).

 

 

  1. Mount the SD Click board, ‘microSD click’ from MikroElektronika (http://www.mikroe.com/click/microsd/) on the mikro bus interface J5.
  2. Insert a micro SD card into the microSD click board card slot.
